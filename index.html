Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is BMI?+
BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It's a simple calculation used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height. It's calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by the square of your height in meters (kg/mÂ²).
How accurate is this BMI calculator?+
Our BMI calculator provides accurate results based on the standard BMI formula. It supports both metric (kg/cm) and imperial (lbs/inches) units, ensuring precision for all users. However, BMI is a general indicator and doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or other factors.
Who can use this BMI calculator?+
This calculator is designed for adults aged 18 and older. It's not suitable for children, pregnant women, or individuals with specific medical conditions, as their BMI interpretations may differ.
What do the BMI categories mean?+
BMI categories are: Underweight (<18.5), Normal Weight (18.5-24.9), Overweight (25-29.9), and Obese (30+). Each category provides a general indication of health risks related to body weight, though individual health varies.
Why does gender matter in the calculator?+
While the basic BMI formula doesn't differ by gender, we include it for future enhancements (e.g., personalized health insights). Currently, it doesn't affect the calculation but helps us tailor user experience.
Can I share my BMI results?+
Yes! After calculating your BMI, use the "Share Results" button to share via your device's sharing options or copy the results to your clipboard for easy pasting.
Is my data stored when I use this calculator?+
No, we don't store any personal data entered into the calculator. Your inputs are processed locally in your browser and cleared when you reset or leave the page. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
